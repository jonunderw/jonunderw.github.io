My Story

Hi, I’m Jon Underwood a Mississippi native, technologist by trade, and lifelong explorer by nature. I grew up in Fulton, MS — a small town about 20 miles east of Tupelo (yes, home of Elvis and Rae Sremmurd). After high school, I attended the University of Mississippi (Ole Miss) where I earned my Bachelors of Science in Computer Science

After college, I worked briefly in Oxford, MS, but in 2018, I made the move to Dallas with my bestfriend, Lola, a Border Collie/Aussie Shepherd mix. Then in 2020, I met my now wife (just got married in September of 2024), and now we’re planning the next chapter of growing our family, in Nashville, TN.

Professionally, I’m driven by systems thinking, hardcore engineering, & orchestrating breakthrough. I love building meaningful products and solutions that sit at the intersection of business strategy, engineering, and human behavior.

I’m currently working on ProductVerse—a space to explore ideas across Product Management, Engineering, and Operational excellence, where I blend insight & research with a touch of philosophy.

When I’m not knee-deep in ProductVerse development or tinkering with coding & new tech, you’ll likely find me (or not find me), recharging outdoors, enjoying remote camping, running, hiking, kayaking, biking, fishing, golfing - you name it, or fueling my curiosity with music and podcasts.

I’m also a strong advocate for wellness, sustainability, and giving back through community and international outreach.

I’m here to build, learn, and leave things better than I found them.
